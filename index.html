About Typing Studio

Welcome to Typing Studio!

Typing Studio is a free online typing practice platform designed to help users improve typing speed, accuracy, focus, and confidence through engaging typing challenges and practice sessions.

Our goal is to make typing practice simple, enjoyable, and accessible for everyone, whether you are a student, professional, or beginner.

Features

  • Typing Speed Practice
  • Accuracy Improvement
  • Timed Challenges
  • Progress Tracking
  • Achievement System

Thank you for using Typing Studio and being part of our learning journey.

Privacy Policy

Typing Studio respects your privacy.

We do not collect personal information such as passwords, banking information, or sensitive personal data.

Typing progress, achievements, and statistics may be stored locally in your browser to improve your experience.

In the future, third-party services such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense may be used to improve functionality and provide relevant advertisements.

By using Typing Studio, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
